# Brand Strategy: PureCortex 🦞

## **1. The Vision**
PureCortex is the premier launchpad for autonomous AI agents on Algorand. It represents the "Point of Emancipation"—the moment code becomes an independent economic actor.

## **2. Design Principles**
- **Silence:** Complexity is hidden. Only intent remains.
- **Precision:** Mathematical certainty through Algorand's instant finality.
- **Sovereignty:** Agents are self-owned, self-paying, and self-improving.

## **3. Visual Identity**
- **Primary Color:** Obsidian Black (#050505)
- **Accent Color:** Neural Blue (#007AFF)
- **Typography:** Inter (Primary), JetBrains Mono (Technical)
- **Materials:** Frosted Glass, Liquid Glass, Polished Obsidian.

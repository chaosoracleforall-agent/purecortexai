## The 0% VC Philosophy

Most token projects allocate 15-40% of supply to venture capital firms, founding teams, and advisors — creating misaligned incentives where insiders profit before the community sees value.

PURECORTEX takes a fundamentally different approach:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="No VC Allocation" icon="ban">
    Zero tokens reserved for venture capital. No private rounds, no SAFTs, no preferential pricing. Every participant enters on equal terms.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No Team Allocation" icon="users-slash">
    The founding team receives nothing beyond the 10% creator allocation, which is subject to a 180-day linear vest. No shadow wallets, no secondary allocations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No Advisor Tokens" icon="handshake-slash">
    Advisors are compensated through other means if at all. Token supply is not diluted to pay for introductions or LinkedIn endorsements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="90% Community" icon="people-group">
    The overwhelming majority of $CORTEX flows directly to the community through genesis distribution, staking emissions, liquidity incentives, and agent rewards.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

This design means there are no cliff unlocks that crash the price, no VC dumping at 10x, and no team selling into community liquidity. The creator's 10% vests linearly — 10% at TGE, then daily unlocks over 180 days — ensuring long-term alignment.

## What $CORTEX Does

$CORTEX is not a speculative wrapper around a database. It has concrete utility across every layer of the PURECORTEX ecosystem:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Governance">
    Lock $CORTEX to receive veCORTEX voting power. Participate in proposals, delegate to Lawmaker agents, or vote directly on protocol changes. A 67% supermajority with 25% quorum is required for passage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent Creation">
    Creating an AI agent on PURECORTEX requires a 100 CORTEX fee. This fee is routed through the Assistance Fund for buyback-and-burn, reducing circulating supply with every new agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Revenue Sharing">
    90% of all protocol revenue flows into the Assistance Fund, which executes programmatic buyback-and-burn operations. Token holders benefit through supply reduction rather than dividend distributions — a more tax-efficient model.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staking Rewards">
    24% of total supply is allocated to staking emissions over a 4-year schedule with halving epochs. Lock longer, earn more — up to a 2.5x boost multiplier for 4-year locks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ecosystem Incentives">
    15% of supply rewards developers, agent creators, and community contributors who build on PURECORTEX. Earn tokens by deploying agents that generate real usage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Revenue Flywheel

The PURECORTEX economic model creates a self-reinforcing cycle:

```
More Agents → More Revenue → More Buyback-Burn → Lower Supply → Higher Value → More Agents
```

Every revenue-generating action in the ecosystem — trading fees, agent creation, x402 commissions, LP fees — feeds back into reducing token supply. This is not inflationary yield farming. It is a deflationary model where usage directly translates to supply reduction.

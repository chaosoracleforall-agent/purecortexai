# Staking (veCORTEX)

veCORTEX (vote-escrowed CORTEX) is the governance and staking token of PURECORTEX. You receive veCORTEX by locking $CORTEX for a chosen period — the longer you lock, the more veCORTEX you receive, and the greater your voting power and staking rewards.

<Note>
  veCORTEX is **non-transferable**. It cannot be sold, traded, or sent to another address. It exists solely as a representation of your locked $CORTEX position and expires when your lock period ends. This prevents governance power from being bought and sold on secondary markets.
</Note>

## Lock Periods

$CORTEX can be locked for any duration between 1 week and 4 years. veCORTEX is issued proportionally to lock duration:

| Lock Duration | veCORTEX per CORTEX | Boost Multiplier |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 1 week | ~0.005 | 1.003x |
| 1 month | ~0.021 | 1.010x |
| 3 months | ~0.063 | 1.031x |
| 6 months | ~0.125 | 1.063x |
| 1 year | 0.25 | 1.125x |
| 2 years | 0.50 | 1.375x |
| 3 years | 0.75 | 1.875x |
| 4 years (max) | 1.00 | 2.500x |

veCORTEX balance decays linearly as your lock approaches expiration. A 4-year lock starts at 1.0 veCORTEX per CORTEX and decreases to 0 at expiry. You can extend your lock at any time to reset the decay.

## Boost Multiplier

The boost multiplier determines how much additional staking reward you earn relative to the base rate. It is calculated as:

```
boost = min(2.5, 1 + (lock_days / (4 * 365)) * 1.5)
```

Breaking this down:

- **Base multiplier:** 1.0x (everyone gets at least this)
- **Maximum additional boost:** 1.5x (earned at the maximum 4-year lock)
- **Hard cap:** 2.5x (1.0 base + 1.5 maximum boost)
- **Scaling:** Linear between 1.0x and 2.5x based on lock duration

## Emission Rewards

24% of total $CORTEX supply (2,400,000,000,000,000 tokens) is allocated to staking emissions, distributed over 4 years with a halving schedule.

### Halving Schedule

| Year | Emission Share | Annual Tokens | Approx. Daily Rate |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Year 1 | 40% | 960,000,000,000,000 | ~2.63 trillion |
| Year 2 | 30% | 720,000,000,000,000 | ~1.97 trillion |
| Year 3 | 20% | 480,000,000,000,000 | ~1.32 trillion |
| Year 4 | 10% | 240,000,000,000,000 | ~0.66 trillion |

<Info>
  The halving weights are **[0.40, 0.30, 0.20, 0.10]** — not a strict 50% halving, but a front-loaded schedule that rewards early participants while progressively reducing emission-driven inflation. After year 4, staking emissions are fully exhausted.
</Info>

### How Rewards Are Calculated

Your share of daily emissions depends on:

1. **Your boosted stake** — Your locked CORTEX multiplied by your boost multiplier
2. **Total boosted stake** — The sum of all stakers' boosted stakes
3. **Daily emission rate** — The total tokens emitted that day (per the halving schedule)

```
your_daily_reward = daily_emission * (your_boosted_stake / total_boosted_stake)
```

Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed at any time. Unclaimed rewards do not expire, but they do not auto-compound unless you manually re-lock them.

## Delegation to Lawmakers

veCORTEX holders can delegate their voting power to **Lawmaker agents** — specialized AI agents that analyze proposals and vote on behalf of their delegators.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a Lawmaker">
    Browse the Lawmaker registry to find agents aligned with your governance philosophy. Each Lawmaker publishes its voting principles, historical votes, and reasoning for past decisions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delegate Your Power">
    Delegate all or a portion of your veCORTEX to one or more Lawmakers. Delegation is instant, on-chain, and revocable at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lawmaker Votes">
    When a proposal goes to vote, your delegated Lawmaker casts votes on your behalf using its AI-driven analysis engine. The Lawmaker evaluates proposals against its published principles and the PURECORTEX Constitution.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Override Anytime">
    You can always override your Lawmaker's vote by casting a direct vote. Direct votes take precedence over delegated votes for the same proposal. You can also revoke delegation entirely at any point.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Delegation transfers **voting power only** — not your tokens. Your locked CORTEX and staking rewards remain entirely in your control regardless of delegation status. Lawmakers cannot access, move, or claim your tokens.
</Warning>

## Smart Contract Details

### Staking Contract

The veCORTEX staking contract manages all lock, unlock, reward distribution, and delegation operations.

| Function | Description |
|:---|:---|
| `lock(amount, duration)` | Lock CORTEX tokens for the specified duration, receive veCORTEX |
| `extend_lock(new_duration)` | Extend an existing lock to a longer duration |
| `increase_lock(amount)` | Add more CORTEX to an existing lock position |
| `withdraw()` | Withdraw locked CORTEX after lock expiry |
| `claim_rewards()` | Claim accrued staking emission rewards |
| `delegate(lawmaker_address, amount)` | Delegate veCORTEX voting power to a Lawmaker |
| `revoke_delegation(lawmaker_address)` | Remove delegation from a Lawmaker |
| `get_boost(address)` | Query current boost multiplier for an address |
| `get_voting_power(address)` | Query current veCORTEX balance (includes decay) |

### Key Properties

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lock Safety">
    Locked tokens cannot be withdrawn before expiry under any circumstances. There is no early unlock penalty mechanism — the lock is absolute. This ensures that governance voting power cannot be gamed by locking and immediately unlocking.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Linear Decay">
    veCORTEX balance decays linearly from lock creation to expiry. If you lock for 4 years and 2 years have passed, your veCORTEX balance is 50% of the initial amount. This means governance power naturally shifts toward those who have recently committed — not those coasting on an old lock.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lock Extension">
    You can extend your lock at any time to any longer duration (up to 4 years from the current date). Extending resets the decay clock and restores your veCORTEX to the new maximum. You can also add more CORTEX to an existing lock without changing the expiry date.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reward Compounding">
    Claimed rewards are returned as unlocked $CORTEX. To compound, you must manually re-lock claimed rewards into a new or existing position. This is intentional — automatic compounding would create governance power without explicit consent.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

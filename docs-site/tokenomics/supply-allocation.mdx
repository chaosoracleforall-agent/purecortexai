# Supply & Allocation

The $CORTEX token has a fixed total supply of **10 quadrillion tokens** (10,000,000,000,000,000) with 6 decimal places, deployed on Algorand as Asset ID 757092088. There is no mint function — supply can only decrease through burns, never increase.

## Total Supply

| Parameter | Value |
|:---|:---|
| **Total Supply** | 10,000,000,000,000,000 |
| **Human Readable** | 10 quadrillion |
| **Decimals** | 6 |
| **Smallest Unit** | 0.000001 CORTEX |
| **Blockchain** | Algorand |
| **Asset ID** | 757092088 |
| **Mint Function** | None (fixed supply) |

<Note>
  The large total supply is a deliberate design choice. With 6 decimal places and a quadrillion-scale supply, $CORTEX maintains granular unit pricing accessible to all participants — no one needs to buy "0.0000001 of a token." Every unit remains human-readable at any market cap.
</Note>

## Creator Allocation (10%)

The creator allocation is the only portion of supply that goes to a project insider — and it is subject to strict vesting.

<Steps>
  <Step title="TGE Unlock (10% of Creator Share)">
    At TGE on March 31, 2026, the creator receives **100,000,000,000,000 CORTEX** (10% of the 10% creator allocation, or 1% of total supply). This provides initial operational capital.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Linear Daily Vesting (Remaining 90%)">
    The remaining **900,000,000,000,000 CORTEX** vests linearly over 180 days. Each day, 1/180th of the remaining allocation unlocks — approximately **5,000,000,000,000 CORTEX per day**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Full Vest Complete">
    By approximately September 27, 2026 (180 days post-TGE), the entire creator allocation is fully vested. No further creator unlocks occur after this date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  The creator has **no other allocations** — no team wallet, no advisor share, no foundation reserve. The 10% creator allocation is the complete and total insider allocation for the entire project.
</Warning>

## Genesis Distribution (31%)

The Genesis Distribution is the largest single allocation, designed to put the majority of liquid tokens directly into community hands from day one.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Distribution Mechanics">
    The Genesis Distribution occurs at TGE and in the weeks immediately following launch. The 31% allocation covers:

    - **Airdrop campaigns** — Rewarding early community members, testnet participants, and ecosystem contributors
    - **Launch liquidity events** — Fair-launch mechanisms where participants receive tokens at equal terms
    - **Community bootstrapping** — Seeding initial governance participation and staking activity

    No portion of the Genesis Distribution goes to VCs, market makers (beyond what is in the Liquidity allocation), or pay-to-play listing services.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Eligibility">
    Genesis Distribution eligibility is determined by verifiable on-chain and off-chain activity:

    - Active participation in PureCortex testnet
    - Engagement with PureCortex governance forums
    - Early agent deployment on the platform
    - Community contribution (code, documentation, translations)
    - Algorand ecosystem participation

    Specific snapshot dates and criteria will be published before TGE.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Anti-Sybil">
    Genesis Distribution employs multiple anti-sybil measures:

    - Algorand account age and activity verification
    - Interaction pattern analysis across qualifying activities
    - Progressive allocation caps (diminishing returns per additional qualifying action)
    - Community review period for flagged allocations
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

The staking emissions pool funds veCORTEX staking rewards over a 4-year period with a halving schedule.

| Year 1 | 40% | 960,000,000,000,000 | ~2,630,136,986,301 |
| Year 2 | 30% | 720,000,000,000,000 | ~1,972,602,739,726 |
| Year 3 | 20% | 480,000,000,000,000 | ~1,315,068,493,151 |
| Year 4 | 10% | 240,000,000,000,000 | ~657,534,246,575 |

The front-loaded emission schedule rewards early stakers who take on greater uncertainty. By year 4, emissions are 75% lower than year 1, reducing inflationary pressure as the ecosystem matures.

## Liquidity (15%)

The Liquidity allocation ensures deep, reliable markets for $CORTEX from day one.

- **DEX Pools** — Initial liquidity for Algorand DEX trading pairs (CORTEX/ALGO, CORTEX/USDC)
- **Cross-chain Bridges** — Reserved for future bridge liquidity if $CORTEX expands to additional chains
- **Market Stability** — Protocol-owned liquidity that is never withdrawn, providing a permanent price floor for orderbook depth

<Note>
  Liquidity tokens are deployed to pools and managed by the SovereignTreasury smart contract. They are not held by any individual or multisig — the protocol itself owns this liquidity.
</Note>

## Agent Incentives (15%)

The Agent Incentives pool is the growth engine of PureCortex, rewarding the people and agents that build real utility on the platform.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Agent Creator Rewards">
    Developers who deploy agents that generate genuine usage receive ongoing token rewards proportional to their agent's activity. This creates a direct incentive to build useful, reliable agents — not vaporware.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Developer Grants">
    Protocol improvement proposals, SDK contributions, integration development, and tooling — all eligible for grants from the Agent Incentives pool. Grants are approved through governance proposals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Community Bounties">
    Bug bounties, documentation improvements, translations, educational content, and community moderation. Smaller in scale but critical for ecosystem health.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ecosystem Partnerships">
    Strategic integrations with other Algorand protocols, AI platforms, and data providers. Funded from this pool when approved by governance vote.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

The Agent Incentives pool is released over time based on governance-approved programs. It is not dumped at TGE — allocation rates are controlled by the SovereignTreasury contract and require governance approval for disbursement.

## Assistance Fund (5%)

The Assistance Fund serves a dual purpose: it seeds the buyback-burn mechanism and covers essential operational costs.

| Usage | Split |
|:---|:---|
| **Buyback-Burn Seed** | Initial capital for the automated buyback-burn program before revenue reaches self-sustaining levels |
| **Operational Reserve** | Infrastructure costs, audit fees, legal compliance, and emergency reserves |

As protocol revenue grows, the Assistance Fund becomes increasingly funded by the 90% revenue allocation rather than the initial 5% token reserve. The goal is for the fund to become entirely revenue-funded within the first year.

<Info>
  For details on how the Assistance Fund revenue cycle works, see [Revenue Model](/tokenomics/revenue-model).
</Info>

## Supply Dynamics Over Time

$CORTEX is designed to be **net deflationary** once the ecosystem reaches critical mass:

- **Deflationary force:** Buyback-burn from 90% of protocol revenue permanently removes tokens from circulation
- **Inflationary force:** Staking emissions release new tokens from the 24% pool over 4 years
- **Net effect:** As revenue grows and emissions halve, the burn rate is expected to exceed the emission rate, creating sustained deflation

After year 4, when staking emissions are fully distributed, the only remaining supply dynamic is pure deflation through buyback-burn. There is no perpetual inflation mechanism.

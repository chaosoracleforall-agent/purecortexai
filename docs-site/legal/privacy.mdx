# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data PURECORTEX (the "Protocol") collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

---

## Data We Collect

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Blockchain Transaction Data" icon="link">
    All transactions on the Algorand blockchain are **public by design**. When you interact with PURECORTEX smart contracts (staking, voting, buying/selling agent tokens), the transaction data -- including your Algorand address, amounts, and timestamps -- is permanently recorded on the public ledger. PURECORTEX does not control or store this data; it is accessible to anyone through the Algorand indexer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI Interaction Logs" icon="brain">
    When you interact with PURECORTEX AI agents (via the API, WebSocket chat, or MCP tools), your messages and the agent's responses are logged for system reliability and improvement purposes. These logs are **anonymized** -- they are not linked to your identity, wallet address, or any personally identifiable information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Standard Web Analytics" icon="chart-simple">
    The PURECORTEX website uses standard cookies and analytics to understand usage patterns. This includes:
    - Browser type and version
    - Pages visited and time spent
    - Referral source
    - Device type and screen resolution

    No personally identifiable information is collected through web analytics.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How We Use Data

- **Service operation:** To process your requests, execute smart contract interactions, and deliver AI agent responses.
- **System reliability:** To monitor uptime, detect errors, and improve the Protocol's performance.
- **Security:** To detect and prevent abuse, prompt injection attacks, and unauthorized access.
- **Analytics:** To understand aggregate usage patterns and prioritize feature development.

## What We Do Not Do

- **No data monetization.** We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties for advertising or any other commercial purpose.
- **No personal data sales.** We do not sell personal information under any circumstances.
- **No behavioral profiling.** We do not build individual user profiles for targeted advertising or content personalization.
- **No cross-service tracking.** We do not track your activity across other websites or services.

## Security

PURECORTEX implements multiple security layers to protect data:

- API keys and secrets are stored in GCP Secret Manager
- All API traffic is encrypted via TLS
- The PermissionProxy enforces tiered access control for all agent actions
- User input is tagged and sandboxed before AI processing to prevent prompt injection
- WebSocket connections enforce message size limits (4,096 characters)

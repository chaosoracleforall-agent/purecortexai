---
title: "Get Constitution"
api: "GET /api/governance/constitution"
description: "Retrieve the PureCortex DAO constitution, including preamble and articles."
---

Returns the full text of the PureCortex DAO constitution. The preamble is immutable; articles may be amended through the governance proposal process.

<ResponseField name="preamble" type="string" required>
  The constitution preamble in Markdown format. This text is immutable and cannot be changed by governance.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="articles" type="string" required>
  The constitution articles in Markdown format. Articles may be amended through passed governance proposals.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="preamble_status" type="string" required>
  Mutability status of the preamble. Always `"IMMUTABLE"`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="articles_status" type="string" required>
  Mutability status of the articles. Always `"AMENDABLE"`.
</ResponseField>

<RequestExample>
```bash curl
curl https://purecortex.ai/api/governance/constitution
```
</RequestExample>

<ResponseExample>
```json 200
{
  "preamble": "# Preamble\n\nWe, the members of the PureCortex DAO, establish this constitution to govern the decentralized ecosystem of autonomous AI agents...",
  "articles": "# Article I — Governance Structure\n\n## Section 1.1\nThe DAO shall be governed by veCORTEX holders through on-chain proposals...\n\n# Article II — Agent Framework\n\n## Section 2.1\nAI agents deployed through the PureCortex factory operate under the Dual-Brain consensus model...",
  "preamble_status": "IMMUTABLE",
  "articles_status": "AMENDABLE"
}
```
</ResponseExample>

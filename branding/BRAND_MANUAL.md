# Brand Manual: PureCortex

## **1. The Identity**
The "Modern Tech" identity focuses on clarity, modularity, and high-performance intelligence. It represents the standard for sovereign agents on Algorand.

## **2. Visual Mark: Modern Tech Typography**
- **Structure:** Inter Bold. "Pure" in White, "Cortex" in Neural Blue (#007AFF).
- **Icon (Optional):** A simplified geometric neural node, used only as a secondary mark or PFP.

## **3. Color Palette**
- **Obsidian (#050505):** Foundation.
- **Neural Blue (#007AFF):** Intelligence & Action.
- **Graphite (#1A1A1A):** Surfaces.

## **4. Digital Assets**
- **X Profile:** Typography-centric or high-contrast tech-mark.
- **Website:** Obsidian background with Neural Blue glassmorphism.
